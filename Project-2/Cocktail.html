Peppermint Espresso Martini

Needed Ingredients

  • Peppermint vodka
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Espresso, brewed and cooled
  • Half-and-half cream
  • Peppermint extract

Directions

  1. Place ice cubes in a cocktail shaker. Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso, cream, and peppermint extract.
  2. Place the top on the cocktail shaker and shake for about 20 seconds until the liquid is very cold.
  3. Strain the cocktail into a martini glass and serve immediately.
